User Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: December 26, 2024

How to Delete Your Data

Team Manager does not permanently store user data beyond your active session. However, if you wish to revoke access:

Option 1: Revoke App Access via Facebook

  1. Go to your Facebook Settings
  2. Click on "Apps and Websites"
  3. Find "Team Manager" in the list
  4. Click "Remove" to revoke access

Option 2: Logout from Team Manager

  1. Log in to Team Manager
  2. Click the "Logout" button
  3. Your session data will be immediately cleared

What Data is Deleted

When you revoke access or logout:

  • Your session authentication token is invalidated
  • No personal data remains in our system
  • You will need to log in again to use the app

Data Retention

Team Manager does not retain user data after your session ends. All authentication is handled by Azure Static Web Apps and Facebook.
