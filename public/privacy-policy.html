Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 26, 2024

Information We Collect

Team Manager uses Facebook Login for authentication. When you log in, we collect:

  • Your Facebook user ID
  • Your name
  • Your email address (if provided by Facebook)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Authenticate your identity
  • Provide access to the Team Manager application
  • Personalize your experience

Data Storage

Your authentication data is managed by Azure Static Web Apps and is not permanently stored in our database. Session data is maintained only during your active session.

Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties except as required by law.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our GitHub repository.
